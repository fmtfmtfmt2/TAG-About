Junior Zookeeper

Conservation Guide

During the January 2013, I learned about a youth volunteering opportunity, available to kids aged 11 to 18, at the Dallas Zoo. I thought it was a wonderful chance to strengthen my love for animals and to gain independence, responsibility, and communication skills. So, I applied, was interviewed, and accepted into the Junior Zookeeper program.

One of the unique things about volunteering at the Dallas Zoo are the "Behind the Scenes tours. Every other week, we are taken behind the scenes of an exhibit, such as the Reptile House or the Chimpanzee exhibit (my personal favorite), or location, such as the Animal Hospital and the Animal Dietary Center.
